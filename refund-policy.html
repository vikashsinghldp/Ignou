Refund & Replacement Policy

IGNOU Study Store

ℹ️ Overview

The majority of our products are delivered digitally (PDF format). Given the nature of digital goods, refunds are generally not applicable once a file has been successfully delivered. However, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction — if there is a genuine issue with your order, we will make it right through our Replacement & Redo Policy. Custom-prepared physical orders are governed by additional terms as outlined below.

✅ Eligible Cases for Replacement or Redo

You are entitled to request a replacement or corrected version in the following situations:

  • An incorrect course, subject, or programme was delivered
  • The PDF file is corrupt, incomplete, or cannot be opened
  • The wrong session's material was delivered (e.g., 2025–26 instead of 2026–27)
  • A scanned soft copy contains pages that are unclear, illegible, or missing
  • A hard copy has missing pages, poor print quality, or a defective binding
  • A Synopsis, Project Report, Dissertation, or Internship File does not follow the agreed structure, format, or content scope communicated at the time of ordering

✅ Upon receiving a valid replacement request, we endeavour to provide the corrected version within 48 hours. Please report issues promptly along with your Order ID and a description of the problem.

🚫 Cases Where Refund or Replacement Does Not Apply

  • The customer provided incorrect course, programme, or enrollment details at the time of ordering
  • A digital file has been successfully delivered and the request is based on subjective dissatisfaction with the content
  • An assignment or project has been rejected by IGNOU — academic evaluation is entirely IGNOU's internal process and is beyond our control
  • The customer provided an incorrect or incomplete delivery address for a physical order
  • The product has already been used or submitted, after which a refund is requested
  • A complaint for a digital product is raised more than 48 hours after delivery
  • The request is based on a change of mind or cancellation without a valid service-related reason
  • A custom hard copy order (Handwritten Assignment or Synopsis/Project/Dissertation/Internship File) is cancelled after the material has been prepared and is ready for dispatch — see special terms below

📘 Custom Hard Copy Orders — Special Cancellation Policy

The following products are prepared exclusively for each individual student based on their personal academic details (name, enrollment number, programme, subject, and topic). As such, they are subject to stricter terms:

Handwritten Assignments — Hard Copy
Individually handwritten based on the student's programme and subject details.
Synopsis | Project Report | Dissertation | Internship File — Hard Copy
Custom-prepared document with Spiral Binding or Diary/Hard Binding as selected at the time of ordering.

The following terms apply to all custom hard copy orders:

  • A minimum advance payment of 50% or more (via UPI) is required before the order is confirmed and production begins
  • The remaining balance is payable before or at the time of dispatch
  • Once the material is prepared and ready for dispatch, no cancellation will be accepted
  • If a cancellation is requested after the material is ready, no refund will be issued — including the advance payment

⚠️ Reason for this policy: These materials are custom-made to each student's specific details and have no use beyond that individual order. Once prepared, they cannot be repurposed or resold. Cancellation at this stage results in a complete loss of materials, labour, and associated costs borne by us — therefore, no refund or replacement can be offered for cancellations made after production is complete.

💸 Refund Processing

In exceptional cases where a refund is determined to be applicable (e.g., confirmed non-delivery of service), it will be processed as follows:

UPI Payments Refund credited to the original UPI ID within 3–5 working days of approval.
Cash on Delivery Refund processed via UPI transfer after return of the physical order is confirmed.

⚠️ Refunds are issued only in cases where IGNOU Study Store has been unable to deliver the agreed service. In all other qualifying cases, replacement or redo of the material is the preferred resolution.
